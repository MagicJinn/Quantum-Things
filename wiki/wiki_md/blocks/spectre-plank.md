# Spectre Plank

## Description

---

The Spectre Plank is crafted from Spectre Logs and is used as a building material and crafting ingredient. It maintains the translucent, ethereal appearance of Spectre materials and can be used in various recipes.

Spectre Planks are essential for crafting Spectre tools, weapons, and other items. They can also be used as decorative building blocks with their unique visual properties.
