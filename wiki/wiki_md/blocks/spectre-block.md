# Spectre Block

## Description

---

The Spectre Block is an unbreakable, translucent block used to construct Spectre Cubes in the Spectre dimension. It cannot be destroyed by explosions or entities and provides the building material for creating personal dimensional spaces.

Spectre Blocks are translucent and work together with Spectre Cores to form the structure of Spectre Cubes. They are essential for expanding and customizing your Spectre dimension space.
