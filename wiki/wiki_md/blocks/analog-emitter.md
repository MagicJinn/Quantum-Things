# Analog Emitter

## Description

---

The Analog Emitter is a block that allows you to easily produce analog redstone signals with a custom strength. If its input side (circle) is powered (strength is irrelevant) it will output a redstone signal of the strength specified in its gui on the other sides (Squares).
