# Magic Hood

## Description

---

The Magic Hood can be worn as a helmet and has 2 effects:

- In Multiplayer your player name does not render, whether you are crouching or not.
- You emit no potion particles.

As armor it has the same properties as chain armor but it is indestructible.

It can only be found in dungeon chests & blacksmith villager chests.
