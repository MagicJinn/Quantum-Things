# Bean Stew

## Description

---

Bean Stew is a hearty meal made from Beans that fills you up better than mushroom stew. It restores 8 hunger points and gives you 4.8 saturation, so you'll stay full longer. When you finish eating, you get your bowl back to use again.

Since beans are easy to farm, Bean Stew makes for a great renewable food source. Once you've got your bean farm going, you'll have a steady supply of this filling meal to keep you going on your adventures.
