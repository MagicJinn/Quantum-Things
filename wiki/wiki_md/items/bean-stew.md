# Bean Stew

## Description

---

Bean Stew is a food item crafted from Beans that provides 8 hunger points and 4.8 saturation, making it slightly better than Mushroom Stew. Eating the stew returns an empty bowl to your inventory.

Bean Stew is a renewable food source that can be easily farmed by growing Bean Sprouts. It provides a good balance of hunger restoration and is a staple food for players who have set up bean farming operations.
